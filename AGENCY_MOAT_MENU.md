# AlmaSEO — Agency-Tier Moat Menu

*Built 2026-05-29 from the completed Product Map audit (195 rows: Pro 135 / Agency 60). Goal: turn the audited table into an action plan for making the Agency tier a compelling, defensible upgrade.*

---

## TL;DR

- **Your Agency tier is not thin — it's buried and unpackaged.** 60 Agency features collapse into **5 coherent value-props**, and one of them (Agency Operations Intelligence) is a genuine moat **no SEO competitor has**.
- The job is **packaging + a few targeted builds**, not starting from zero.
- **Lead with "run your SEO business," not "more SEO."** Pro already wins on AI content; Agency must win on *operations*.

---

## Part 1 — The moat as it stands (the 60 Agency rows, reframed)

Five value-props. Each has a marketing one-liner you could ship today.

### 1. 🟢🟢 Agency Operations Intelligence — *the crown jewel*
> *"AlmaSEO watches your team and your retainers like a COO — flagging burnout, scope creep, and silent clients before they cost you money."*

Work Intelligence suite (**Client Silence Detection, Burnout & Compression Detection, Time Sink & Scope Creep Detection, Momentum Tracking, Work Category Breakdown**) + Work Timer, Work Schedule Preferences, Daily Pacing Briefing, Topbar Stats Ticker (+ actual-rate / utilization / revenue slides).

**Moat: unique.** No SEO tool monitors team health, scope creep, or client neglect. This is the single most differentiated thing in the whole product. *It should be the headline of the Agency tier.*

### 2. 🟢 Retainer & Profitability Management
> *"Know your real effective hourly rate on every client, in real time — and never blow a retainer budget again."*

Retainer Tracker, Monthly fee per client, **Effective rate (fee ÷ hours)**, Utilization %, Monthly revenue, Retainer budget overage, Retainer Summary (Agency-Wide), Retainer Gamification & Milestones, Retainer Settings Change Log, Last Billing Period.

**Moat: strong.** Profitability-per-client is rare; most agencies track it in spreadsheets.

### 3. 🟡 Client Reporting & Communication
> *"One-click branded client reports — what you did, what it's worth, sent from your domain."*

Client Report Builder + CC / BCC / Reply-To, Email Report Sender (SendGrid), removable line-items, remove buttons.

**Moat: table-stakes but solid.** Every agency tool has reporting; yours is tied to real logged work, which is a quality edge.

### 4. 🟢 Multi-Client Command Center
> *"Every client's pulse on one screen — activity, revenue, what needs attention."*

Cross-site Activity Feed, Business Overview Card (budget rates + revenue), Retainer Summary (Agency-Wide), Strategy-tasks-due widget.

**Moat: strong.** The cross-client birds-eye view is a real agency need.

### 5. 🟡 Strategy & Delegation
> *"Build a phased SEO game plan per client, generate writer-ready briefs, and let reminders run the cadence."*

Strategy Game Plan (Phases, Completions, scheduling options ×6, Templates, Categories & Priority, edit/delete, Completion criteria), Automated reminders (+ lead time, on-hold toggle), **Generate Content Brief Only**.

**Moat: good.** Phased per-client strategy + briefs-for-writers is genuine delegation infrastructure.

### Cross-cutting: 🤖 AI Concierge (Alma Chat)
The 23-tool conversational assistant — positioned as an Agency perk (also contains its real OpenAI cost). A "your AI ops manager" angle.

## Part 3 — Net-new feature shortlist (prioritized: impact × effort)

1. **Per-client profitability dashboard** — 🟢 HIGH impact / 🟢 LOW effort. *Do this first.* You already compute effective rate, utilization, revenue, and budget — just aggregate them into one "P&L per client" view. Cheap, and it makes value-prop #2 undeniable.
2. **White-label client portal** — 🟢 HIGH / 🔴 HIGH. Read-only client login showing their reports, activity feed, and wins. The strongest single Agency-upgrade driver, but a real build.
3. **Proposal / audit-to-pitch generator** — 🟢 HIGH / 🟡 MED. Turn a First Impression + SEO Recovery Review into a branded sales proposal PDF. Reuses existing AI; turns your audit tools into a *sales* weapon (huge for agencies winning clients).
4. **Team seats + task assignment** — 🟢 HIGH / 🟡 MED. Assign Strategy/Project tasks to teammates; make Work Intelligence per-person. Unlocks true multi-seat Agency pricing.
5. **Client approval flows** — 🟡 MED / 🟡 MED. Client signs off on content/strategy before it ships.
6. **Capacity planner** — 🟡 MED / 🟡 MED. Forward-looking team-hours vs. committed retainer work.

> **Watch-point (your own rule):** don't let these leak into Pro. Each is a "manage clients/team" capability — keep them Agency-exclusive or the moat dissolves.

## Part 4 — Pro→Agency elevation: a "Local SEO at Scale" bundle

A second potential moat. These Pro rows are local-business / multi-location plays:

`City/County/State Page generation` · `Locations` · `Business Profile (GBP)` · `AI Review Response Drafting` · `GBP Search Queries → Recommendations` · `Reviews → Content Mining` · `GBP Testimonials in articles` · (+ `NAP Shield`, already Agency)

**Idea:** package these as an Agency "Local SEO at Scale" module — multi-location clients are agency bread-and-butter, and the AI review/GBP mining is genuinely differentiated.

**⚠️ Caution:** a solo local-business owner (Pro) reasonably needs *basic* local SEO. Don't strip all local from Pro. Keep **single-location basics in Pro**; gate **multi-location management + the AI GBP/review mining** to Agency. That's the clean line.

## Part 5 — Packaging recommendation

- **Free** = the WordPress plugin's local SEO hygiene (the WP.org acquisition funnel).
- **Pro** = "grow ONE site with AI" — content engine, AI intelligence, single-site analysis.
- **Agency** = Pro + "run your SEO **business**" — the 5 operations value-props above + Local-SEO-at-Scale + the AI Concierge.

**Marketing order for the Agency page:** lead with **#1 Operations Intelligence** (the unique hook), then #2 Profitability, then #4 Command Center. Reporting/Strategy are supporting proof, not the headline.
