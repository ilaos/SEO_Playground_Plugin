# Bulk Metadata Editor

Update SEO titles and meta descriptions across multiple posts at once. Find and replace, append, prepend, or reset metadata in batch — saving hours of manual editing.

---

## How It Works

The Bulk Metadata Editor loads a paginated, filterable table of all your posts with their current meta titles and meta descriptions. You can edit individual entries inline or apply bulk operations to multiple posts at once.

---

## Editing Metadata

### Inline Editing

Click on any post's meta title or description field in the table to edit it directly. Changes are saved when you click away or press Enter.

### Character Limits

AlmaSEO shows character counts and warns when you exceed recommended limits:

| Field | Recommended | Max Display |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| **Meta Title** | 65 characters | 580px in search results |
| **Meta Description** | 160 characters | 920px in search results |

---

## Bulk Operations

Select multiple posts and apply one of four operations:

### Reset

Clear the meta title and description for all selected posts. Useful for starting fresh or reverting to auto-generated defaults.

### Append

Add text to the **end** of the existing field. For example, append ` | {site}` to add your site name to all titles.

### Prepend

Add text to the **beginning** of the existing field. For example, prepend `{year} Guide: ` to add the current year to titles.

### Find & Replace

Search for specific text within the field and replace it. For example, replace "2024" with "2025" across all meta titles.

---

## Dynamic Placeholders

Bulk operations support these placeholders that expand automatically:

| Placeholder | Expands To | Example |
|-------------|-----------|---------|
| `{site}` | Your site name | "My WordPress Site" |
| `{year}` | Current year | "2026" |
| `{month}` | Current month name | "March" |
| `{day}` | Current day | "1" |
| `{category}` | Post's primary category | "SEO Tips" |

---

## Filtering

Use the filters above the table to narrow your selection:

- **Post Type** — Posts, pages, or any public custom post type
- **Status** — Published or draft
- **Taxonomy** — Filter by category, tag, or custom taxonomy
- **Term** — Specific term within the selected taxonomy
- **Date Range** — Posts published between two dates
- **Search** — Filter by post title
- **Missing Meta** — Show only posts missing a meta title or description
